About Us

Aloha from the Team

Founded in 2010, we are a team of certified instructors dedicated to sharing the stoke of surfing. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refine your cutback, we ensure safety and fun in every session.

Our methodology focuses on reading the ocean, understanding wave mechanics, and building confidence in the water. We don't just teach you to stand up; we teach you to surf.
